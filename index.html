AGRO STAR

AGROSTAR is a rich organic fertilizer prepared by anaerobic and aerobic decomposition of agricultural residues in plants. Each 100 gm of Agrostar contains 3 crore helpful microorganisms that help in nutrient absorption from the soil.

Dosage: 1-2 gm per liter of water
